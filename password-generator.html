🔐 Password Generator – Strong, Unique & Secure

Weak or reused passwords are the easiest way for hackers to break into your accounts. A Password Generator creates random, complex passwords that are hard to guess.

Why use a Password Generator?

  • Protects against brute‑force and dictionary attacks
  • Prevents reuse across sites
  • Fast, free, and easy

How to use (Step‑by‑Step)

  1. Choose length (12–16 recommended).
  2. Include uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols.
  3. Click Generate and copy the result.
  4. Save in a password manager.

Best Practices

  • One password per account
  • Enable two‑factor authentication (2FA)
  • Change weak/old passwords

FAQs

Is it safe? Yes—good tools generate locally and don’t store results.

What length is best? 12–16+ with mixed characters.
