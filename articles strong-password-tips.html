Strong Password Tips

Apna account secure rakhne ke liye hamesha strong password use karo. Ye kuch tips hain:

  • Kam se kam 12 characters ka password banao.
  • Uppercase, lowercase, numbers aur special symbols mix karo.
  • Common words ya apna naam/number use na karo.
  • Har website/app ke liye alag password rakho.
  • Password manager use karna best option hai.
