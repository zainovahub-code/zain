What is BMI?

BMI (Body Mass Index) is a simple calculation using a person’s height and weight. It is used to quickly determine whether you are underweight, normal, overweight, or obese.

How to Calculate BMI?

The formula is:
BMI = Weight (kg) / Height (m²)

BMI Categories

  • Underweight: BMI < 18.5
  • Normal weight: 18.5 – 24.9
  • Overweight: 25 – 29.9
  • Obese: BMI ≥ 30

Why is BMI Important?

BMI is an easy screening tool to estimate body fat and related health risks. However, it does not measure body fat directly. For athletes and children, other measures may be more accurate.
